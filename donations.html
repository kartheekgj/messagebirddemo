Message Bird

Accepting donations to support one of their 3 initiatives: Marine Life, Earth Life, Air Life

  • Anyone can reach out to one of the channels to start a donation
  • Chatbot asks a handful of questions to validate and classify the donation
  • Chatbot presents a payment link to the user
  • Upon the success of the payment, all the information gathered should be registered in our BI tool
  • Reporting Worldwide of Animals in Danger and Animal Abuse.
